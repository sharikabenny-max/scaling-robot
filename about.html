About

Fun ideas,
carefully built.

I'm Sharika — an illustrator and writer based between sketchbooks and Google Docs. I trained in animation and comic book illustration and have spent the years since figuring out how to use my gift for art in crafting cheerful, meaningful work for clients and personal projects alike.

My process usually starts on paper before I move on to a digital medium to make the finished artwork. I'm drawn to soft and vibrant colors, clear shapes, and small human details that make each artwork tell a story.

When I'm not drawing, you'll find me before an open laptop working on my novels, for I love to write just as much as I do drawig. My love for visual storytelling carries through from sketchbooj to paper.
